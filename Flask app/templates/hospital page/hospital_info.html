    Financial Assistance for Brain Tumor Treatment

    We understand that brain tumor treatment can be a heavy financial burden. Below are various avenues to get financial help, from government schemes to insurance plans and charitable organizations.

    Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY)

    Government's flagship health insurance scheme, offering cashless treatment for economically disadvantaged people.

    Learn More

    Cancer Patients Aid Association (CPAA)

    CPAA provides financial assistance for cancer and brain tumor patients, including surgeries, medications, and travel expenses.

    Learn More

    Medical Crowdfunding

    Platforms like Ketto, Milaap, and GoFundMe allow individuals to raise funds for medical treatment through public donations.

    Ketto | Milaap

    Health Insurance Providers

    Leading insurance companies like Star Health, Religare, and HDFC ERGO provide comprehensive health insurance for neurological treatments.

    Star Health Insurance | Religare Health Insurance

    National Health Mission (NHM)

    NHM provides financial aid for cancer and other life-threatening conditions. Many states provide cashless treatment under NHM schemes.

    Learn More

    Government Hospitals Financial Aid

    Many public hospitals offer reduced rates or free treatment for low-income individuals. Contact hospitals like AIIMS, SGPGI for more details.

    AIIMS Delhi | SGPGI Lucknow

    Employer Assistance

    Many employers provide medical assistance or health insurance to employees. Check with your company's HR department for available options.

    Charitable Foundations

    Some foundations, like the Tata Memorial Centre, provide funds and support for cancer patients, including brain tumor treatments.

    Tata Memorial Centre

    Hospital Financial Aid Programs

    Some hospitals, such as the Max Healthcare Group, offer discounts or payment plans for those undergoing brain tumor treatment.

    Max Healthcare
